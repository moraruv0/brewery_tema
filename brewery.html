Ingredients*

1.5 OZ ---------- OF BITTER LIQUEUR, CAMPARI

4 OZ ---------- FRESH ORANGE JUICE

1 ORANGE SLICE FOR TOPPING

*

PLEASE NOTE THAT
THESE INGREDIENTS
PRODUCE A
MAGNIFICIENT LINEAR-
GRADIENT(#EDB14C,
#B63B30)

Long story short

An old English proverb says 'early to bed and early
to rise make a man healthy, wealthy and wise', but
what do we do once we're awake... During the
Christmas season, we only hve one word for you:
Garibaldi. This cocktail only has two ingredients
and is perfect at any time be it brunch or cocktail
hour.

Steps

  1. SQUEEZE THE JUICE OF TWO ORANGES AND POUR THE JUICE INTO
    A BLENDER.
  2. MIX UNTIL AN AERIAL TEXTURE IS OBTAINED.
  3. POUR THE CAMPARI INTO A GLASS FILLED WITH ICE
  4. TOP WITH AN ORANGE QUARTER.
